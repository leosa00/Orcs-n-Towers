Firstly makes sure that the target enemy still exists, if it doesn\textquotesingle{}t the missile is destroyed. If the enemy still exists it checks whether or not the missile has hit it, if there\textquotesingle{}s been a hit, the missile is destroyed. If the missile has not hit the enemy, it re-\/calculates its directional vector, based on its and the target enemy\textquotesingle{}s current positions, and moves towards the target.
